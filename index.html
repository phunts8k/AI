What This App Does

AI Generated Questions

Every session gets a completely fresh set of questions from Gemini AI. No two sessions are the same.

Real AI Feedback

Your answer is read and evaluated by Gemini AI, which gives you a score and specific improvement tips.

Session Tracking

Your scores are saved between sessions so you can track your improvement over time.

Performance Review

After all questions, AI writes a personalised review covering your strengths and areas to improve.

How It Works

1. Create an account on the Sign Up page.

2. Log in with your email and password.

3. Go to your Dashboard and click "Start Interview".

4. Enter your free Gemini API key to activate AI.

5. Answer each AI-generated question in the chat.

6. Receive a full AI performance review at the end.
